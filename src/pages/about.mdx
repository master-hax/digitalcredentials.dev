# About digitalcredentials.dev

digitalcredentials.dev is maintained by members of the [W3C Federated Identity WG](https://www.w3.org/groups/wg/fedid/), with contributions from members of the OpenID Foundation's [Digital Credentials Protocols WG](https://openid.net/wg/digital-credentials-protocols/) and members of the [FIDO Alliance](https://fidoalliance.org).
